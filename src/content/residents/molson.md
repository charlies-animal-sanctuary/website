story: >-
  Molson was the best dog in the world. He came to me at about a year and a
  half old, after living as a stray. The first time we met, he walked
  straight over, leaned his whole body against my legs, and looked up at me.
  His foster dad said, "He never does that. That's your dog." He was right.


  Molson was loveable, goofy, and impossibly chill — an old soul even when he
  was young. He loved food (all of it), loved to swim, loved people, loved
  kids, and eventually even loved the cats. Mostly he loved being near his
  humans, asleep against someone's leg.


  He would want this rescue to help dogs like him — the sweet, gentle ones
  who just need a home. His toys are still here, still getting used. He was
  part of my heart.
