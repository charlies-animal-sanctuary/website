# Your admin guide — running the sanctuary site

Everything you'll ever do lives at one address:

**charlies-animal-sanctuary.pages.dev/admin** *(sign in with GitHub)*

Whatever you publish is live on the website about two minutes later,
automatically. You can't permanently break anything — every change is saved
in history and can be brought back.

---

## Adding an animal for adoption

1. **Adoptable animals** → **Add** (or "New Animal").
2. Fill in what you know. Only the starred fields are required — anything
   else can stay blank, it's fine.
3. **Bio: write as the animal.** "I'm a two-year-old couch potato who…" —
   first person, your voice, like the ones already on the site.
4. **Photo:** straight off your phone is perfect. The site automatically
   shrinks it for visitors — never resize anything yourself. Tall (portrait)
   photos look best on the cards.
5. **Save/Publish.** Two minutes later they're on the Adopt page with an
   apply button.

Tick **"Feature on homepage"** for the one or two animals you most want seen.

## When someone applies

Applications, surrender requests, and contact messages all arrive in the
sanctuary Gmail inbox. Replying to the email replies straight to the person.

## The status field is the whole system

- **Available** — in the Adopt grid, with an "Apply to adopt" button.
- **Pending** — stays in the grid with an "Adoption pending" pill; the apply
  button hides itself.
- **Adopted** — moves to Happy Tails automatically. Set the **Adopted date**
  when you flip it, so the wall stays in order.

**Adopted animals are never deleted.** Happy Tails is your proof-of-wins
wall — it only ever grows. That growing list is what makes strangers trust
you with adoptions and donations.

## The homepage photo strip

**Photo highlights** → add a photo + a short caption. To make a tile click
through to Instagram: open your post → share → **Copy link** → paste it into
the Link field. Around 6–12 tiles looks best. Swap them whenever you like.

## Our Family

Same as animals, in the **Our family** collection. The **"In loving
memory"** switch moves someone to the "Forever in our hearts" section.

## Deleting — the one careful thing

Deleting is ONLY for something you yourself just created by mistake (like a
test entry). One thing to know: confirming a delete removes the entry **and
its photos** in one go — there's no picking and choosing — and if a photo
happens to be shared with another entry, the site can break until it's
repaired.

So the rule is simple: **unless it's a fresh mistake of your own, message
Faraaz instead of deleting.** A cleanup takes him seconds, everything is
saved in history, and remember — adopted animals are never deleted at all;
they're your Happy Tails wall.

## If something looks wrong

Published something and it hasn't appeared after ~5 minutes? Don't re-do
it — screenshot what you did and message Faraaz. Every version of everything
is saved, so nothing is ever lost.
